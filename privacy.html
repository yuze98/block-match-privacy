Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

01

Overview

Block Match ("the Game") is developed and operated by Youssef. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game on Android.

We are committed to being transparent about data practices. Block Match does not require account registration or collect any personally identifiable information directly.

02

Information We Collect

Block Match itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. However, the third-party advertising SDK integrated into the game may collect certain information automatically:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, device model, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level)
  • App usage and interaction data for ad targeting
  • Network information (carrier, connection type)
03

Advertising – Unity Ads / LevelPlay

Block Match uses Unity Ads (via IronSource LevelPlay) to display advertisements. This SDK may collect and process data to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Unity's data collection and usage is governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Unity Privacy Policy
  • IronSource / LevelPlay Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings under Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

04

Data We Do Not Collect

To be clear, Block Match does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Photos, microphone, or camera data
05

Children's Privacy

Block Match is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the game, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

06

Data Retention & Security

Since Block Match does not collect personal data directly, we have no personal data to retain or delete. Any data collected by third-party SDKs (Unity Ads / LevelPlay) is managed according to their respective retention policies.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the game after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, feel free to reach out.
